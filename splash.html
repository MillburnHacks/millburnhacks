What is Splash?

Children in elementary & middle school are invited to Millburn Splash to have fun learning chess, math, dancing, and much more! All the classes are taught by Millburn High School students. Each child can be accompanied by one parent at no cost. Tickets are only $20, and all money goes to fund clubs in Millburn High School. You can pay in cash at the door.
