What is Millburn Splash?

Millburn Splash is a program where children in elementary and middle school can come to learn and have fun! All classes are taught by high school students with a wide array of interests, so there's always something for everyone!

There are 4 hours, and 5 classes are taught each hour. You can decide which one to take when you come, but feel free to take a look at the current class list!

How do I come?

Before you come, please make sure to register. Tickets cost $20 per child and you pay at the door. All money raised goes to fund Millburn High School clubs. Each child must have a separate ticket. Up to one parent per family may come free of charge.
