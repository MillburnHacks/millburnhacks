hackMHS III Info

What is hackMHS?

hackMHS is Millburn High School's hackathon. The first hackMHS was in October of 2015, and the second was in May of 2016. A hackathon is a programming competition in which prizes are given, but there are also lots of fun side-activities! Everything is free, including food, shirts, and more.

Who can come?

All high school students are invited! You don't have to have programming experience or have gone to a hackathon before. If you're in college or older, we would love it if you could register as a mentor.

When is it?

It's on Saturday, December 3rd, from 9am to 9pm. We will post a full schedule soon.
